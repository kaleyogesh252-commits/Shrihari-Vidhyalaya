Shrihari Vidhyalaya School

With a vision to be the top international school in Beed, Universal Shrihari Vidhalaya School (SVS) is a premium. international school in Beed that offers International Curricula. The School is located in Wahegaon,, Beed, and is backed by the Vishwakarma Purple Educational Trust and the prestige of Shrihari World Schools. SVS, Beed, aims to empower students to become global citizens, prepared to face any challenge and committed to making the world a better place. SVS brings the most rigorous international standards and approaches that foster international mindedness, deeply rooted in Indian values.

Our Multiple Intelligence Tree

UWS’s ‘Multiple Intelligence Tree’, inspired by Howard Gardner, facilitates our mission to bring out the unique talent of each child. Each child has multiple intelligences, which will be honed along with his/ her intellectual capability.

  • Naturalistic
  • Linguistic
  • Logical (Mathematical)
  • Spatial
  • Physical (Kinesthetic)
  • Musical
  • Inter-personal skills and
  • Intra-personal skills
will lead to their holistic development.

UWS's vision, ‘Nurturing Learners for Life’, is depicted by the trunk and branches of our multiple intelligences and shows the path for learners and the support they will receive in becoming the best version of who they are.

Legacy

The Wisdom World schools are amongst the most awarded schools in India. All Wisdom World Schools are QS-iGauge Platinum-rated schools (the highest achievable rating),that puts our schools on par with international standards. We also are an IGBC Platinum-rated Green Schools Campus. Hence, we walk the talk when we claim to be a truly International School of high standards.

Our first Wisdom World School at Wakad, Pune, established in 2010, is one of the most prestigious and sought-after Schools in Pune. Each year, our CISCE board results break all records, and our students achieve top ranks not only in Pune city but also in the whole country. Our Principal, Ms. J. Simoes, is a multi-award winner (National Awards for Teachers and the prestigious Derozio Award) and is amongst the best educationists in the country. She is also a mentor for many Teachers and Principals alike. Little wonder then that the school saw phenomenal growth and grew with over 2,200 students in a very short span of 5 years.

After reaching great heights, it was time to establish the second Wisdom World School at Hadapsar. In 2015, the foundation stone for Wisdom World School, Hadapsar was laid. WWS Hadapsar, too, saw exponential growth with the dedication and commitment of our team of experienced and capable staff. Our first batch results of the CISCE examination stand testimony to what we claim, with a majority of our students scoring above 90%. Vishwakarma International Schools is now focused on setting up and promoting one of the top international curriculum schools in Pune.
