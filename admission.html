Admission Process

Student Interaction

The interaction at Shrihari Vidhayalaya School (SVS) is designed to be pleasant and comfortable for both students and parents. It is conducted by the Head of Admissions, Principal, and Vice Principal to understand the child's educational journey, interests, communication skills, social behaviour, and unique talents.

Primary school candidates may be asked to perform simple activities, while students applying for Grade 5 and above participate in an interaction along with a written assessment in Basic English and Mathematics.

Admission During the Year

Parents may register at any time during the academic year for admission to Shrihari Vidhayalaya School. We welcome applications throughout the year, subject to seat availability.

Mid-term admissions are available for eligible students, ensuring a smooth transition into our learning environment.

Special Program

Pre-term Admission

The Pre-term Program at Shrihari Vidhayalaya School is specially designed for students transferring from educational boards such as CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and others.

This 6 to 8-week program begins in April and helps students smoothly adapt to the new curriculum, teaching methods, and school environment, ensuring a confident transition.
