INFRASTRUCTURE

As an international school in Balewadi, Pune, the infrastructure and facilities support the holistic education that the School imbibes. Students can leverage state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities like STEM labs and sports installations to pursue artistic and co-curricular disciplines.

  • Smart Classrooms: Equipped with modern digital boards for interactive learning.
  • Advanced Labs: Fully functional Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and STEM/Robotics labs.
  • Sports Facilities: Spacious playgrounds, basketball courts, and indoor sports areas.
  • Library & Resource Center: A vast collection of books, journals, and digital learning resources.
